Private Equity

Master leveraged buyouts, portfolio management, and value creation

What is Private Equity?

Private equity professionals invest in and manage private companies, using leverage to maximize returns. They focus on operational improvements, strategic growth, and value creation over a 3-7 year investment horizon before exiting through a sale or IPO.

PE careers typically require 2-3 years of investment banking experience. The work involves deal sourcing, financial modeling (especially LBO models), due diligence, portfolio company management, and ultimately driving returns for limited partners (LPs) who invest in PE funds.

Core Skills

LBO Modeling

Build leveraged buyout models analyzing purchase price, debt structure, operational improvements, and projected returns (IRR and cash-on-cash multiples).

Due Diligence

Assess company operations, financials, market position, competitive dynamics, and identify red flags before making investment decisions.

Portfolio Management

Monitor and improve portfolio company performance through board involvement, strategic initiatives, and operational enhancements.

Deal Sourcing

Identify and evaluate investment opportunities through proprietary channels, investment banks, brokers, and industry relationships.
